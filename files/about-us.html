About Us

Enabling a Safer Society and Smarter Living

Overview

PVC Security (PVC) is a premier authorized dealer and system integrator at the forefront of the AIoT security industry. Our mission is to bring world-class machine perception and intelligence solutions to every sector. By partnering with global technology leaders, we provide a comprehensive portfolio of surveillance, access control, and intelligent traffic solutions that empower businesses and individuals to thrive in a safer environment.

Since our inception, PVC has achieved significant growth, continuing its upward trajectory in the security market. We are committed to technological excellence, working closely with the R&D institutes of our global partners to bring the most advanced Big Data, Cybersecurity, and Smart City innovations directly to our clients.

With in-depth insights into the smart IoT landscape, we explore emerging opportunities across machine vision, robotics, video collaboration, and smart fire safety, ensuring our integrations stay ahead of the curve.

PVC Technology acts as a pioneer in the industry to propose the "Ai-First" concept in the intelligence era. We have built an open Video AI Cloud Platform to tap into the value of data for smart city operations. Our solutions cover transportation, government, retail, banking, logistics, and energy sectors, delivering end-to-end efficiency and security.
